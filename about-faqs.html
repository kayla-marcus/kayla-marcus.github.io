Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Welcome to Checkmate Tutoring's FAQs page! Here, we've compiled answers to some of the most common questions you might have about our online tutoring services. If you don't find the information you're looking for, please feel free to reach out to us directly.


Q: What subjects and topics do your tutors cover?

A: Our expert tutors cover a wide range of subjects, including but not limited to mathematics, science, language arts, history, and test preparation. We offer tutoring for various grade levels and academic levels.


Q: Are the tutoring sessions conducted online?

A: Yes, all our tutoring sessions are conducted online. Tutors host 1-on-1 or group sessions through platforms like Zoom, Google Meets, or other video chat platforms for a personalized learning experience.


Q: How do I schedule a tutoring session?

A: Scheduling a session is easy! Simply visit our website, log in to your account, and choose your desired subject, tutor, and session time. You can then proceed to book the session and make the payment online.


Q: Can I choose between 1-on-1 and group tutoring sessions?

A: Yes, you can choose between 1-on-1 and group tutoring sessions based on your preference and learning goals. Group sessions are a cost-effective option where you can study with peers who are learning the same topic.


Q: What are the prices for tutoring sessions?

A: Our tutoring sessions are competitively priced, and the cost may vary based on the subject, level, and type of session (1-on-1 or group). For specific pricing details, please visit our Pricing page on the website.


Q: How do I make a payment for the tutoring session?

A: We accept cash or checks mailed to 306 Progress Way Willmar, MN, 56201. Other methods include digital transcations using Venmo, Cash App, and Apple Pay. Payments can be sent to contact@checkmate-tutoring.com or (320)-212-9623.


Q: Can I reschedule or cancel a tutoring session?

A: Yes, you can reschedule or cancel a tutoring session at any time. To do so, call (320)-212-9623 or contact@checkmate-tutoring.com to let your tutor know about the cancellation.


Q: Are there any additional materials or resources provided during the tutoring sessions?

A: Yes, our tutors often provide additional study materials, resources, and practice exercises to enhance your learning experience. These materials are tailored to your specific needs and help reinforce the concepts covered during the sessions. If you would like to learn more about the resources our tutors provide, visit our Resources page on the website.


Q: How can I contact customer support for further assistance?

A: If you have any more questions or need assistance, you can reach our customer support team through email at contact@checkmate-tutoring.com or by filling out the contact form on our website. You can also call us at (320)-212-9623. We aim to respond to your questions within 24 hours.


Thank you for considering Checkmate Tutoring for your educational needs! We look forward to helping you succeed in your academic journey.
