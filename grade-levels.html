Grade Levels

Checkmate Tutoring provides comprehensive academic support from pre-K to college, fostering success through personalized guidance, boosting confidence, and nurturing growth.

Elementary Levels

Checkmate Tutoring recognizes the most important parts of elementary school in our services. We help build foundational skills such as reading, writing, and math, while also fostering growth of socialization and a love for learning.

Middle School Levels

Checkmate Tutoring helps pre-teens grow in the academic and personal development areas of life. Some key aspects include building strong study habits, social skills, and self-discovery.

High School and Above

We believe high school and higher education are critical periods in students' lives. We help them navigate through rigorous academics and develop important skills needed for success.
