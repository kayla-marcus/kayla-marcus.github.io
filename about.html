Our History

Empowering Minds, Enriching Futures: The Checkmate Tutoring Story

Welcome to Checkmate Tutoring, where education meets inspiration! Founded 8 years ago with a vision to make quality education accessible to all, Checkmate Tutoring has been a beacon of learning, support, and growth for countless students and communities.

Our Humble Beginnings

In 2015, Checkmate Tutoring was born out of a passion for education and a desire to make a difference. Our founder, Jolene Relane, recognized the need for personalized tutoring and mentoring services to help students thrive academically. With a handful of dedicated tutors and a commitment to excellence, Checkmate Tutoring embarked on a journey to transform lives through education.

A Legacy of Impact

Over the past 8 years, Checkmate Tutoring has had the privilege of working with over 55 students, guiding them on their educational journeys and empowering them to achieve their goals. Our dedicated team of tutors, mentors, and support staff have played a crucial role in shaping the future of these young minds.

Giving Back to Education

At Checkmate Tutoring, we believe in the power of education to create positive change in the world. That's why we are proud to have donated over $5,000 to various education charities and initiatives. These contributions have supported classrooms, provided educational resources, and made learning opportunities accessible to underserved communities.

Celebrating Achievements, Inspiring Futures

As we celebrate our 8th anniversary, we reflect on the countless success stories that have emerged from the heart of Checkmate Tutoring. Our alumni have gone on to excel in their studies, pursue higher education, and make meaningful contributions to society. Each achievement is a testament to the dedication of our team and the potential within every student we serve.

Our Commitment Continues

Looking ahead, Checkmate Tutoring remains committed to fostering a love for learning, nurturing individual talents, and making education a transformative experience for all. We are excited about the future and the opportunity to continue making a positive impact on the lives of students and communities.

Thank you for being a part of our journey. Together, we can create a brighter, more educated future for all.
